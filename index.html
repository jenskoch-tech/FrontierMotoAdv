The Concept

This is not a tour company. It is a private invitation.

We scout the routes, ride the terrain, and share what we find with a small group of capable riders. No guides. No support vehicles. No hand-holding. Just good tracks, shared and enjoyed together with the right people.

  • Self-guided concept
  • Max 3 riders
  • Shared GPX files
  • Bivouac or local hotels depending on weather

The Route

    Difficulty: Intermediate

    Style of Travel

    We travel light, ride hard, and stay flexible. No fixed hotels. No daily check-ins. If the track is worth staying for, we stay. If the weather turns, we adapt. That is the point.

    Wild Camping

    Preferred whenever possible. We find the quiet spots away from the crowds.

    Flexible Pace

    No fixed schedule. If we find a great track, we stay. If we need to push, we push.

    About Us

    "Frontier Moto Adventure started as a personal obsession with finding routes that don't show up on tour company brochures. After years of overland travel across Europe — from the Alps, to the Pyrenees to the Adriatic coast — the format is simple: scout a route, ride it properly, and invite a small group of riders who can handle what the terrain throws at them."

    Come ride with us

    Think you are the right fit? Tell us what you ride, where you have ridden, and which dates work for you. We will be in touch within 48 hours.

    Important Disclaimer

    This is a self-guided ride. Every rider is solely responsible for their own safety, decisions, and equipment. We share the route and the timeframe with you, but you are riding independently.

    Frontier Moto Adventure is not a commercial guided tour operator. There are no guides, no support vehicles, no fees and no medical staff. You must be self-sufficient.
